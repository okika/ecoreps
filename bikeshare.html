EcoReps Bike Share Committee

EcoReps Bike Share members train Columbia students on the basics of navigating New York roads responsibly and emissions-free!

"Columbia Bike Share provides Columbia students with everything they need to bike throughout New York city. Wear your helmets!"

Currently, six working bikes are provided for all Columbia students as a healthy, free, and environementally friendly transportation alternative. Please sign up for the Bike Share program here, and an email will be sent out to you regarding training sessions. Bikes are available for check-out from the Hartley Hospitality Desk from 8 AM to 7 PM. You must attend a training sessions and sign the waiver to be eligible for Bike Share.

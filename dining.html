EcoReps Dining Committee

Dine green with our committee!

"The Dining Committee takes its meals seriously - and sustainably! Always make sure to minimize your food waste by serving yourself appropriately!"

The Dining Committee focuses on introducing sustainable measures in all of Columbia's dining halls. Reducing food waste is a priority, and bimonthly plate scrapings have been used to promote awareness of this issue. With the plate scrapings, waste has been reduced by dozens of pounds. Other initiatives have included decreasing the availability of non-reusable utensils and dinnerware, and promoting the use of local and sustainable food sources. If you have suggestions for other sustainable initiatives in dining, do not hesitate to email Shawnee Traylor (snt2118@columbia.edu) or Sue Yoon (sy2422@columbia.edu). For more information, check out our Ecoreps TV in the John Jay Dining Hall above the conveyor belt!

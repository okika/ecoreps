EcoReps Living Green Committee

Living Green helps Columbia students develop green habits and live sustainably!

"The Living Green Committee wants you to help you recycle, flip off the light switch, and minimize your footprint."

The Living Green Committee is committed to promoted green living throughout Columbia's campus. This takes form in their plastic bag recycling intiatives, awareness campaigns, and their new merchandise store. The Living Green Committee also collaborated with Columbia Housing to kick off the CCN Energy Challenge and also headed the planning of our annual Greenfest spring festival.

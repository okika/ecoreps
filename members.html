EcoReps Executive Board

Hello!

The EcoReps Board thanks you for visiting our website and learning more about our efforts to make Columbia a greener and more sustainable campus. We are proud to present our cause to the public and hope you will join in. If you would like to work with us, offer suggestions, or ask any questions, feel free to reach out to us and we will get back to you as soon as possible! Thanks again!

Sincerely,
EcoReps Eboard

Emma Tuzinkiewicz

Coordinator

Emma Tuzinkiewicz is a Junior in Columbia College majoring in Sustainable Development and Hispanic Studies. Before she was Co-President of EcoReps, she was Co-Leader of the Dining Committee. Emma loves working with her fellow EcoReps and other members of the Columbia community to help them lead eco-friendly lives. Outside of EcoReps, Emma is Co-President of EcoLions, a member of Kappa Alpha Theta, and has volunteered with a nearby public school. She enjoys exploring the city and running in Riverside Park!

Kelly Echavarria

Coordinator

Kelly is a sophomore in Columbia College majoring in economics with a probable concentration in sustainable development. Before she became president she was a Living Green committee head and loved the fact that the committee worked so closely with students in residence halls. Outside of EcoReps, Kelly enjoys doing community service with a club called Circle K and planning events for residents in John Jay, where she is an RA. She also liked enjoying as much time in the sun as possible when it is warm, because she's originally from Texas where it is warm practically all year round!

James Geddis

Treasurer

James is a sophomore studying Earth and Environmental Engineering and is interested in the management and development of our natural resources. His interest in the environment started from a young age. He would often go on all sorts of outdoors adventures with his family, and in high school he was able to attend a school focused on environmental science. He has been able to draw on these experiences during his time as treasurer and as a member of our composting committee. In his free time, James loves to explore the great outdoors in the state of New York. During the school year he enjoys venturing into Central Park, but whenever he can he makes the journey to the Adirondack Park in upstate NY. In addition to EcoReps, James is also a part of COOP, A2E2S, Greenborough, and CU Ski Team. A fun fact about James is that he leaves his windows open year round, and some even say he enjoyed the brutally cold winter this year.

Akiko Shimizu

Secretary/Media Coord

Akiko is a sophomore studying Earth and Environmental Engineering. Originally from Southern California, Akiko was inspired to attend Columbia and study Earth and Environmental Engineering growing up beneath the seemingly perpetual LA smog. She is also a member of the Composting Committee and in her free time she enjoys fiddling with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. Beyond EcoReps she is a members of Scientists and Engineers for a Better Society as well as the American Academy of Environmental Engineers and Scientists.
